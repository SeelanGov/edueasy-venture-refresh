Recognition of Prior Learning with EduEasy

Have work experience but no formal qualifications? EduEasy and Thandi help you use Recognition of Prior Learning (RPL) to earn NQF qualifications—all in one place!

What Is RPL?

RPL validates your skills and experience from work or informal learning to award formal qualifications (NQF Levels 2–7). It’s perfect for adult learners or workers seeking career advancement.

RPL Examples

Here’s how RPL works in different sectors:

Sector Example NQF Level Provider
Construction Experienced builder earning NQF 4 NQF 4 CETA
Customer Service Call centre worker earning NQF 4 NQF 4 Services SETA
Management Team leader earning NQF 5 NQF 5 SAQA

Eligibility

  • Experience: At least 3–5 years of relevant work or informal learning.
  • Documents: Provide work records, references, or portfolios.
  • Tip: Use Thandi’s RPL quiz to check if you qualify!

How to Apply with EduEasy

Follow these steps:

  1. Assess Eligibility: Take Thandi’s RPL quiz at edueasy.co.za.
  2. Gather Evidence: Upload work records, CV, or certificates to EduEasy’s secure platform.
  3. Apply: Submit via SAQA, SETAs, or EduEasy’s portal for select programs.
  4. Track with EduEasy: Monitor your RPL application for free. Upgrade to EduEasy Premium for priority support and funding alerts.

Funding Your RPL

  • SETA Funding: Some SETAs cover RPL assessment costs.
  • NSFAS: Funding may apply for further studies post-RPL. Apply at nsfas.org.za.
