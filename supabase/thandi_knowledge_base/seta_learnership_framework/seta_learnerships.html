SETA Learnerships with EduEasy

SETA learnerships offer practical training and job opportunities in fields like IT, construction, and agriculture. With EduEasy, Thandi helps you find, apply, and track learnerships—all in one place!

What Are SETA Learnerships?

Learnerships combine classroom learning and work experience, leading to NQF qualifications (Levels 2–6). They’re perfect for job-ready skills and often include a stipend.

Popular Learnerships

Here are examples of SETA learnerships:

Learnership Sector NQF Level SETA
Call Centre Operations Customer Service NQF 4 Services SETA
Construction Skills Construction NQF 3–5 CETA
Agricultural Management Agriculture NQF 4–5 AGRISETA
Renewable Energy Skills Energy NQF 4–6 EWSETA

Eligibility

  • Education: Grade 10–12, depending on the learnership. Some require specific subjects (e.g., Maths for construction).
  • Age: Usually 18–35 for youth programs.
  • Tip: Use Thandi’s career quiz to match your Matric to learnerships!
