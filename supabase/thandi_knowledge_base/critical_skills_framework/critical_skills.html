High-Demand Careers with EduEasy

South Africa needs skilled workers in healthcare, engineering, IT, agriculture, and more. EduEasy and Thandi help you explore critical and scarce skills, match them to your Matric, and connect to initiatives like PYEI—all in one place!

What Are Critical and Scarce Skills?

These are high-demand jobs prioritized by the government to grow the economy. Choosing these careers boosts your job prospects and aligns with programs like the Presidential Youth Employment Initiative (PYEI).

Government Initiatives

  • Presidential Youth Employment Initiative (PYEI): Offers training and jobs in critical skills. Sign up at sayouth.mobi via EduEasy.
  • UIF Labour Activation: Funds training for unemployed youth. Explore with Thandi.

How to Prepare

  • Match Your Matric: Careers like teaching need English (50%+). Take Thandi’s free career quiz to check your subjects.
  • Choose a Path: Study at a university (NQF 7) or TVET college (NQF 4–6) via EduEasy.
  • Get Funded: Apply for NSFAS with Thandi’s guidance.
